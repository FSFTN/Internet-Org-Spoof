She’s grown up around some pretty amazing things.

A puppeteer.

A fire dancer.

A stilt performer.

Imagine what Neesha will do.

So where will
Neesha’s magic
take her? 

She’d be happy to keep you posted, but you’ll have to find her first. Because her family is not on the Internet, and that’s
a shame.

Does Neesha really need the Internet? Maybe. Perhaps now, or in the years to come, the Internet will create opportunities for her that she’d never have otherwise. Who knows.

But there’s no question that the Internet needs Neesha—her ideas, her experiences, her perspective. Because Neesha has grown up seeing people achieve the incredible, and that’s exactly the kind of magic we can all believe in.

4.5 billion people have no access to the Internet, and like Neesha,
many of them are children. Get them and their families online
and who knows what kind of magic they’ll create.

Neesha has seen things we can only dream of.

She’s still discovering who she is and what she wants to do.

The internet will give Neesha opportunities she doesn’t even know she has.

Inspiration can come from anywhere. For Neesha, it’s everywhere.

Neesha’s Father has some impressive tricks. But let’s get Neesha online and see some real magic.

A better world needs a better internet. A better internet needs everyone.

Connecting Neesha
to her future

Neesha isn’t connected yet. But once she is, think of all the new things
she’ll learn. Think of what she’ll teach the rest of us.
